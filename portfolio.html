Year 1 Analysis

This All About Me Page was the first website I had made in my first year of the program. I had created this site in order to apply the different languages I had learned and see what I could make with my creativity. The basis of this page was to introduce myself and include simple facts about myself. With this information I was able to organize it in a way that was visually appealing itself on the webpage. After making this website and progressing throughout the program, I used this to learn how I could improve in future websites.

In this project, I decided to base it off the topic of Arizona. I had more skills using bootstrap and with different effects for photographs and text. I was able to apply these new skills to the website and compare this website to the first site I had made and see my improvements. Additionally, this page allowed me to create multiple pages of information and not just one lengthy page. From this project, I learned that having more skills in coding allows you to become more creative and give you more opportunities in what you can do with a website.

With this travel brochure, it had brought everything we learned in year to a close. All together, it had represented the three coding languages we had learned that year which were bootstrap, HTML, and CSS. All of the skills we had learned from each language had incorporated and we were able to create a well developed final site. From this website I learned how to incorporate all of my skills and how these three languages can make up the foundation of a website and their significance.

Year 2 Analysis

After a week of learning new coding skills, these skills get to be applied to websites created every week! These weekly websites give us a fun opportunity to be creative and go out of the box for these sites. While also giving us more creative opportunities, it gives us the ability to practice our web design skills, ensuring that we will not fall behind. Creating these weekly websites only sets us up for success. Throughout the production of these websites, I have learned how to take these newly learned skills and apply them to any typical site.

This website was one of the most interesting websites I have ever made. I absolutely loved having the opportunity to create it as if I had a fake client I was working for. This website had been made for a fake company called Little Bird Toys for them to "utilize" for their business. In the process of making this site, I learned how to operate and adjust to the clients standards, expectations, and needs for the website. This site has by far been the most professional product that I have made this year. I can't wait to experience the SLE mock experience later in the year where I can create another site with similar expectations. With being in the second year of the program, this website has been by far the most effective and influential out of all of the ones I have created.

This year, this animation was one of the more unique things that I have made. With this project, I had the opportunity to learn how to use CSS in other ways for styling, such as creating different aspects into animations. I found this assignment to be more on the fun side and I loved being given a lot of freedom with this.
